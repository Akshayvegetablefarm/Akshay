About Us

Welcome to AKSHAY VEGETABLE FARM! Located in Kapilvastu Municipality, Ward No 9, Kapilvastu District, Nepal, we pride ourselves on growing the finest, organic vegetables. Our farm is managed by Akshay Kumar Barai, who is dedicated to ensuring that each product meets the highest standards of quality. We use sustainable farming practices to enrich the soil and ensure our vegetables are fresh and nutritious.

Our Vegetables

At AKSHAY VEGETABLE FARM, we grow a diverse range of vegetables to cater to all your needs. Our offerings include:

  • Tomatoes - Juicy, ripe, and full of flavor, perfect for salads and cooking.
  • Carrots - Crisp and sweet, great for snacking or adding crunch to dishes.
  • Potatoes - Versatile and hearty, suitable for baking, boiling, and frying.
  • Lettuce - Fresh and crunchy, ideal for salads and sandwiches.
  • Bell Peppers - Colorful and versatile, great for stir-fries, salads, and grilling.
  • Cucumbers - Refreshing and hydrating, perfect for salads and pickles.
  • Spinach - Nutrient-rich and leafy, ideal for smoothies, salads, and cooking.
  • Onions - Essential for flavoring, whether caramelized, raw, or cooked.
  • Radishes - Spicy and crunchy, great for adding zest to salads.
  • Beans - Tender and protein-rich, excellent for soups, stews, and stir-fries.

Our Quality

Our commitment to quality is at the heart of everything we do. At AKSHAY VEGETABLE FARM, we ensure that our vegetables are grown without harmful chemicals, maintaining their natural flavor and nutritional value. We focus on sustainable practices, including organic fertilizers and natural pest control, to enhance soil health and produce high-quality vegetables. Our rigorous quality control ensures that every vegetable meets our standards before it reaches your table.
